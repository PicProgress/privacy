PicProgress Privacy Policy

1. Introduction

Welcome to PicProgress! We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting it through our compliance with this policy. This policy is valid from October 2, 2023.

2. Information We Collect

PicProgress is designed to be a personal tool, and as such, we do not collect any personal information from our users. The only data we collect is anonymous usage data to help us understand how our app is being used and how we can improve it.

3. Camera Access

Our app requires access to your device's camera. This access is solely used for the purpose of allowing you to take photos and add them to your albums within the app. We do not store, share, or have access to any of the photos you take. All photos remain on your device unless you choose to share them.

4. Analytics

We use an analytics tool in our app to help us understand how our users interact with the app. This tool collects anonymous usage data, such as how often certain features are used, but does not collect any personal information.

5. Information Sharing

We do not share any information with third parties. As stated above, we do not collect personal information, and all photos taken within the app remain on your device.

6. Changes to Our Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. Thus, you are advised to review this page periodically for any changes.

7. Contact Us

If you have any questions or suggestions about our Privacy Policy, do not hesitate to contact us: picprogress.app@gmail.com .
